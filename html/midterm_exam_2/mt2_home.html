Welcome to the Agora Gallery! Our main goal is to provide unwavering support to Sedona's thriving graphic arts community. Immerse yourself in the world of fine art while enjoying the inviting atmosphere of our café, where you can always find steaming hot coffee and tea, soothing jazz tunes, and delightful scones.

Rest assured that all the coffee we serve in our café adheres to fair trade and organic standards. Additionally, we proudly present an array of specialty coffee beverages, an assortment of teas, and a selection of wines. As you explore our gallery, indulge in our scrumptious sandwiches, wraps, and soups. To enhance your visit, we provide complimentary and secure Wi-Fi exclusively for our valued customers.

If you're seeking a venue for your upcoming event, think of the Agora Gallery. We're fully equipped to cater to a variety of occasions, including receptions, corporate meetings, wedding celebrations, and more. Our space can accommodate up to 120 guests.

ATTENTION ARTISTS: We're in a perpetual quest for emerging and captivating talents, and we're excited to have the opportunity to review your work. Don't hesitate to reach out to us to arrange a meeting where we can evaluate and appreciate your portfolio.

Spotlight

Having been a consistent presence in the Sedona art scene for an extended period, we are delighted this month to showcase a comprehensive collection of Aza Deere's creations in the exhibition titled Visions of Light & Shadow. Aza embarked on her journey as an artist-in-residence back in 1962, arriving with minimal funds in one pocket and a handful of brushes in the other. Over the years, her brushes have seen much use, and her financial resources have seen gradual growth. Aza's artistic endeavors are renowned for their captivating and ephemeral nature, skillfully intertwining light and shadow in unexpected and enduring manners. We invite you to join Aza on April 7th for a reception commemorating her life's work and artistic contributions.
