Coming Up at the La Seizieme Theater

September is set to bring a remarkable series of events to the La Seizieme Theatre. The Broadway Touring Company is staging three captivating shows of the acclaimed West Side Story, a Tony Award-winning musical. Tickets are in high demand, prompting you to secure yours promptly.

Experience a delightful Date Night with the Chamberlain Symphony, offering a special repertoire of classical melodies tailored for romance. Following that, immerse yourself in intellectual engagement by attending a presentation of Charles Dickens' timeless work, The Mystery of Edwin Drood. The challenge lies in deciphering Dickens' intricate enigma, as the story remains unfinished.

Jazz enthusiasts have every reason to rejoice this September. On the 22nd, the Jazz Masters will grace the stage with their mesmerizing performance. Additionally, September 25th presents an opportunity to savor the musical legacy of The Duke, as the Jazz Company of Kansas City pays tribute to Ellington.

Prepare for a whirlwind of excitement at the Chamberlain Civic Center in September. Witness the astounding artistry of the Taiwan Acrobats on the 3rd of the month. As the month concludes, brace yourself for a visit from the Madtown Jugglers, who seamlessly blend comedy, juggling, and an element of delightful chaos.

For a refined Sunday experience throughout September, indulge in a classical brunch accompanied by the soothing melodies of the Carson Quartet. As seating is limited, we recommend securing your table in advance to avoid disappointment.
